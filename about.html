Biography

Mahbubur Rahman is a dedicated Lecturer in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the Green University of Bangladesh (GUB) since March 28, 2022. Holding M.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Jagannath University (JnU), Mahbubur brings a solid academic background to his role.

In addition to teaching, Mahbubur serves as Program Coordinator of the Day Batch of Department of CSE, Green University of Bangladesh, and Mentor Moderator for the CSE Day Batch. Through these roles, Mahbubur actively fosters student development and academic excellence beyond the classroom.

With a passion for education and a commitment to student welfare, Mahbubur continues to inspire and empower the academic community at GUB.
